KUCSA Terms and Conditions
Membership Requirements:

1. Must be a current student or alumni from Kakamega county of a recognized institution in Kenya.

2. Must adhere to the values and objectives of KUCSA.

Membership Benefits:

1. Access to all KUCSA events and programs.

2. Networking opportunities with other members.

3. Eligibility for leadership positions after one year of active membership.

Membership Fees:

1. Annual membership fee of KES 100 for students.

2. Alumni may choose to pay annual fees or make voluntary contributions.
