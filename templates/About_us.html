About KUCSA

KUCSA (Kakamega Universities and Colleges Students Association) is an official registered organization platform that brings all Kakamega elites together for a common course of "UNITY OF PURPOSE".

The organization brings together all students in all tertiary institutions (Universities, Colleges, Polytechnics) across Kakamega County and Beyond. At the moment, we have managed to register and recruit up to 3000 students from various institutions.

Our Vision

To impact the communities where we live and beyond with other diverse cultures, in addition to our attained goals, skills, and training received during the period of membership, thereby building an all-rounded society that celebrates and enjoys the human resource by Kakamega County.

Our Mission

To bring all Kakamega County students together in unity, move together rising against all odds to impact the community positively.

Our Objectives

Unity of purpose for all Kakamega county comrades in various institutions of Higher learning.
Bring together and promote mutual understanding in all aspects that affects its members.
Promote the welfare of its members, aid members at times of need and or emergencies through material and moral assistance.
Liaise and foster cooperation between the members and various groups and institutions.
Present itself back to the local community and those from the same area but different institutions to foster unity and cooperation.
Promote the participation of members to county programs and activities, projects and development to inculcate the spirit of patriotism and nationalism.
Discuss and outline our political stand as elites/youths from Kakamega county.
